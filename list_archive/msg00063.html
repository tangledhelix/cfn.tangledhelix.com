I'm working for Verio (like Dan)... but I took a management (well duped into what is really a supervisory job) down here in their Austin, TX office. I manage a small team of ColdFusion developers soon to be BEA developers. We also do work in Interwoven.
I actually wanted to move to Austin after I was here on a few projects ... warmer weather, friendly people ... no more snow! The town is small enough for me (I hate crowds) but big enough to have culture and normal city amenities. It is after all the capital of Texas.
A bit of trivia ... the Texas State Capital dome is larger than the US, it's the only state in the Union that is. There's supposedly a law that says no state capital dome can be bigger/taller than the US one ... but we really are our own country down here. I heard also that the Texas constitution has a provision in it that it is allowed to leave the Union at will. :-)
And in San Jancinto they have a monument (very much like the Washington monument, obelisk whatever) - but again it's taller than the Washington one. Muhaha.
