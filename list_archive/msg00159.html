Ok so I guess this is when I take the mic and remind everyone who I am
and let you all know what I've been doing since I was that guy and who
I've become now that I'm no longer he who you once knew. Or I could just
throw some devil horns and go WOOOOOO a lot and pretend like the party
never stopped. Hmmm...

In a day and age long ago a person who's identity has since been lost
amidst the sands of time mistyped my name and through a rube roldgerbian
concoction of wackiness and sandy-timelinss I became DrDaByGoD. Then I
went on from such promising beginnings to piss away my teenaged years
in a fury of awkwardness and gangliness. After which I went to college
and got fat.

Now I'm this bloke who spends his evenings in coffee shops smirking at the
other laptop users who came for the free wireless and the mochas; I came
for the cheap coffee and the words. In the last five years I've written
one novel which I'm seeking an agent for, I've written a second novel
which is still in zero draft form (a zero draft is a draft which is so 
awful it hasn't even negotiated for itself the laudable status of first
draft), and a handful of short stories which I'm seeking to get published
in esteemed literary journals such as "Ploughshares" or "Uncle Max's
Hand-Cranked Basement Garage Quarterly". I have no cats and with every
word with which I'm refusing to end this e-mail I'm probably wasting
another moment of your time. 

It's also true I maintain some presence on the web--I know some links
were posted earlier as I avidly read the archives of this list as if
they were the literal plot synopsis of Memento and they might make the
right now make sense. I don't remember the 90's very well and I'm sad
to say that's not because of a drug-abuse orgy but rather because I've
just got no memory to speak of. I still have no cats.

 Darby
