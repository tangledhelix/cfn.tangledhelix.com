> Oh and Mike - I am going to Hawaii (cruise) for 2 weeks, leaving this Sunday (the 6th of March) - we're pretty much hitting all the islands, and I'd rather do my own adventuring rather than go on the cruise excursions - any Must Sees on your list?   I need some input :)

Well, I'm pretty much an Oahu guy because that's where I lived.  If
you're going to all the islands then the stuff I'd recommend on Oahu
is most likely fairly tame.  Oahu is the tourist trap with Waikiki.  I
think I went to Waikiki once or twice in the 8 months I was there.  I
enjoyed the North Shore the best especially during the winter because
you can literally get knocked over by the friggin' waves.  A bit
scary, but fun nonetheless.  Sunset Beach or the Banzai Pipeline were
my favorites up on the North Shore.  Ko Olina is on the western part
of the island and is a bit "resort," but they have some really nice,
quiet, and small beaches.  Oh, and you can swing by my old house in
Kapolei and let me know how she looks.  I miss her. :)  I've only been
to the other islands for military training, so I've got no clue how
they really are.  Watch out for the Hawaiians.. they don't like us
haole (white people) folk.

And if everyone is wondering why I am posting so much even though I am
in Afganistan it's hard to explain, but I really have an "office" job
out here.  Got my own computer and connection through the Army network
here.  I am, unfortunately, out on the "front lines," but it's really,
really hard to comprehend if you've a) never been in the Army and b)
never been deployed in a combat zone.  So, don't worry -- I am still
defending you guys. :)
